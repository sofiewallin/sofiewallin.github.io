Introduction

Hi! I'm Sofie Wallin and I design and code websites with the user in mind. I've been a professional web designer for 10+ years. Most recently, I've been working as design lead and front-end developer, in a broad role spanning from UX to front-end.

At the moment, I am looking for a new job and on my wish list is an employer who shares my passion for the web, that can offer high technical competence and who also understand the importance of user experience. I'm above all interested in UX/UI-design, front-end development, usability and accessibility. I would love to work in a role that let's me do it all, but I can also see myself working either as a designer or as a developer.

Do you share my passions and interests? Could your company be a good fit for me? I would love to hear from you!

Skills and qualities

I always design with a purpose. My style is minimalistic, even conventional and I always start with the users point of view. In the end I want the websites I create to be usable and accessible with a clearly conveyed message. When I code I focus on the same simplicity and accessibility. In every aspect of the websites I create I want usability, whether it be in the browser or in my code base.
